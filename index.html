Years of Craft

The Invisible
Architecture of Scent

01

Sourcing

We travel to the origin of each ingredient — the oud forests of Laos, the rose fields of Isparta, the frankincense groves of Oman.

02

Composition

Our master perfumer blends each accord by hand. Each formula undergoes months of refinement before it earns the Scentara name.

03

Maturation

Every fragrance rests for six months in glass vats. Time is our most important ingredient — it deepens and unifies the accord.
